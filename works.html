Designing Dashboards

2020 Trip

Designing effective dashboards involves prioritizing user needs and clear objectives, organizing information logically, and using simple, consistent, and visually clear design elements. The most important data should be placed prominently and be immediately accessible, adhering to the "five-second rule," and visualizations should be chosen specifically to represent the data clearly without clutter.

Vibrant Portraits of 2020

2025 Artificial intelligence is changing the rules of the marketing game

"Vibrant portraits of 2020" refers to artistic works that captured the energy, emotions, and complexities of the year, often through striking colors and styles, despite the challenges of the pandemic and social upheaval. Prominent examples include Alexis Franklin's digital renderings, Martin Satí's energetic, line-driven portraits, and artist Zhong Lin's daily lockdown portraits that brought brightness to the year.

36 Days of Malayalam type

2020 Trip

"36 Days of Malayalam type" is not a standard project, but it likely refers to artists interpreting the Malayalam alphabet within the global "36 Days of Type" challenge, where participants create designs for each letter and number over 36 days. You can find many examples of these interpretations by searching for "#36daysoftype" and variations that include "Malayalam," or by exploring Behance and Instagram for posts specifically from Indian artists who have participated.
